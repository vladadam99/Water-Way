## Homepage priority
- Hero: clear value proposition.
- Featured categories: help customers self-select quickly.
- Trust section: quality, UK focus, expert guidance, water efficiency.
- Featured products: once catalogue has images.
- Newsletter: capture early customers.

## Smart collection strategy
Smart collections use product tags. Every product must have at least one department tag and one product-type tag.

## Product page strategy
Product pages should answer:
- What does it do?
- Who is it for?
- What does it fit?
- What are the key specs?
- What is included?
- When should the customer contact Water Way before ordering?
