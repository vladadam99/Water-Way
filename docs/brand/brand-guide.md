# Water Way Brand Guide

## Brand name
Water Way

## Taglines
Primary: **Smarter Water. Better Living.**  
Secondary: **Every Drop Matters. Every Solution Starts Here.**  
Ecommerce header: **Smarter water solutions for every home, garden and business.**

## Positioning
Water Way is a UK-focused specialist store for products that help customers save water, improve water quality, irrigate efficiently, collect rainwater, clean water-using systems, and protect property from leaks.

## Audience
- Homeowners improving taps, showers, appliances and filtration.
- Gardeners and allotment owners looking for efficient watering.
- Landlords and property managers reducing water waste and leak risk.
- Small businesses needing practical water-management products.
- Trades and facilities teams looking for components, filters, pumps and fittings.

## Voice
- Practical, calm and expert.
- Clear rather than clever.
- No exaggerated claims.
- UK spelling.
- Explain compatibility, installation and maintenance plainly.

## Visual identity
- Deep Blue: `#0B5FA5`
- Navy: `#073B6D`
- Aqua: `#22B8CF`
- Light Grey: `#F5F8FB`
- Soft Aqua: `#E8F5FB`
- Text: `#102033`

## Logo direction
A simple water-drop and flowing path mark, paired with a strong wordmark. The starter SVG is included at `assets/water-way-logo.svg`.

## Customer promise
Water Way helps customers choose reliable water products with clear guidance, practical compatibility notes and honest product information.
