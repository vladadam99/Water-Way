# Water Way Supplier and App Plan

## Supplier route

Water Way should use a mixed supplier model:

1. Automated marketplace supplier for early catalogue testing.
2. Direct UK and EU trade suppliers for stronger margins and reliability.
3. Specialist niche suppliers for filtration, irrigation, rainwater and plumbing ranges.

## Priority supplier candidates

### Automated catalogue and fulfilment
- Avasam: UK dropshipping platform with Shopify integration.
- Syncee: supplier marketplace with UK and EU supplier coverage.
- BigBuy: EU product catalogue and Shopify dropshipping option.
- AppScenic: dropshipping and catalogue automation platform.

### Direct trade candidates
- Vyair: water filtration and replacement filter candidate.
- Osmio Water: water filtration and drinking water product candidate.
- Easy Garden Irrigation: irrigation, drip watering and garden watering candidate.
- BES: plumbing fittings and water accessories candidate.
- Wolseley: trade plumbing and water-system product candidate.

## Recommended Shopify apps

### Catalogue and supplier operations
- Matrixify: bulk import, export and migration.
- Stock Sync: stock and price feed automation.
- One supplier connector: Avasam, Syncee, BigBuy or AppScenic after account choice.

### Trust and conversion
- Judge.me: product reviews.
- Shopify Inbox: live chat.
- Klaviyo: email marketing.

### Sales channels
- Google and YouTube channel.
- Meta channel.
- TikTok channel.

## Owner action required

App installation, app billing approvals, supplier contracts and supplier account approvals must be completed from the Shopify owner account or supplier portals. These are account-level actions, so they are queued here rather than silently installed.

## Next supplier decision

Recommended first test route: start with Avasam or Syncee for UK-friendly supplier discovery, then add direct specialist suppliers once the product categories and margins are validated.
