# Shipping Policy

This shipping policy is a starter template and should be reviewed before launch.

## Shipping locations
Water Way is initially focused on customers in the United Kingdom. Additional regions may be added later.

## Processing times
Orders are normally processed after payment is confirmed. Processing times may vary depending on product availability, supplier fulfilment and warehouse operations.

## Delivery times
Estimated delivery times should be shown at checkout or on the product page where available. Delivery estimates are not guaranteed unless specifically stated.

## Large or heavy items
Some products, such as pumps, tanks, reels or rainwater equipment, may require special shipping arrangements or additional delivery time.

## Split deliveries
Orders containing products from multiple suppliers or warehouses may arrive in separate deliveries.

## Incorrect address
Customers are responsible for providing a complete and accurate delivery address. If an order is returned due to an incorrect address, additional shipping charges may apply.

## Delivery issues
If a parcel arrives damaged, customers should contact Water Way as soon as possible with photos of the packaging, product and shipping label.
