# Terms and Conditions Starter Notes

Use Shopify's built-in terms template and review it before launch.

The final terms should cover:

- store ownership and contact details;
- product descriptions and availability;
- pricing and payment;
- order acceptance;
- delivery;
- returns and refunds;
- warranties and limitations;
- customer responsibilities for compatibility and installation;
- acceptable use of the website;
- governing law.

This file is not legal advice and should not be used as the final terms without review.
