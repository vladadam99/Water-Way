# Frequently Asked Questions

## What does Water Way sell?
Water Way sells water-saving products, filtration products, irrigation equipment, pumps, rainwater harvesting accessories, leak-detection products, water testing tools, cleaning products and related water accessories.

## Are your products suitable for UK homes?
Water Way is built for the UK market. Product pages should always be checked for fitting sizes, compatibility notes and installation requirements before purchase.

## Can you help me choose the right product?
Yes. Customers should contact Water Way with photos, measurements, product model numbers or installation details when unsure about compatibility.

## Do filtration products remove every contaminant?
No single filtration product removes every possible contaminant. Product pages should clearly state the filter type, intended use and any standards or performance details provided by the supplier or manufacturer.

## Do water-saving products reduce pressure?
Some water-saving products reduce flow rate while maintaining comfortable performance. Results vary depending on mains pressure, plumbing and existing fittings.

## Do you sell to trade customers?
Water Way plans to support trade and business customers. Trade accounts can be offered later for plumbers, property managers, facilities teams, landscapers and commercial users.

## Do products come with installation instructions?
Where available, product pages should include installation notes or links to manufacturer instructions. Some products may require a qualified installer.

## Are products in stock?
Stock should be confirmed through Shopify inventory or supplier integration. Draft products should not be sold until supplier availability is confirmed.

## How do I return a product?
Returns are handled according to the Water Way returns policy. Products must normally be unused, complete and in original packaging unless faulty.

## How do I contact Water Way?
Use the Contact page and include your order number, product name and any relevant photos or measurements.
