# Privacy Policy Starter Notes

Use Shopify's built-in privacy policy generator and review it before launch.

The final policy should explain:

- what customer data Water Way collects;
- how orders and payments are processed;
- how Shopify and payment providers handle data;
- how marketing emails are managed;
- how cookies and analytics are used;
- how customers can request access, correction or deletion of data;
- the business contact details for privacy enquiries.

This file is not legal advice and should not be used as the final privacy policy without review.
